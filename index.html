RULES
The rules for water color sorting are:
1.Pour into the same color: You can only pour water into a tube if it has the same color as the water you are pouring and there is enough space in the tube.
2.Plan your moves: Think strategically and predict your moves to avoid getting stuck.
3.Restart levels: You can restart a level at any time if you get stuck.
